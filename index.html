Zettelman

Turn appointment notes into organized events.

Zettelman is an iPhone app that captures handwritten or printed medical and appointment notes, extracts the important details, and helps you save them with reminders and calendar support.

English

Use the English landing page for search engines and visitors looking for an AI-powered appointment scanner for iPhone.

  • Scan handwritten or printed appointment notes
  • Extract date, place, and purpose automatically
  • Save reminders and add appointments to your calendar
