Zettelman

Turn event details from any image into organized reminders and calendar events.

Zettelman is an iPhone app for people who receive event details in everyday formats such as doctor slips, email screenshots, invitation cards, and printed notes.

Image-to-event extraction Reminder + calendar flow English and German support Mobile-first on iPhone

Why Zettelman stands out

Beyond doctor slips

Use one workflow for many sources: email screenshots, invitations, letters, printed schedules, and appointment notes.

Human-in-the-loop

AI extraction is fast, but you stay in control: review and edit important fields before saving.

Bilingual SEO pages

Dedicated English and German pages improve discoverability for both language audiences.
