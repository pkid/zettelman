Zettelman Deutsch
iPhone appointment organizer

Scan an appointment note and turn it into a saved event.

Zettelman helps you capture handwritten or printed appointment notes, extract the essential details, and keep everything organized with reminders and calendar integration.

What Zettelman does

  • Capture a medical or appointment note with your iPhone camera
  • Extract date, time, location, and purpose from the note
  • Review the parsed details before saving
  • Create reminders and optionally add the appointment to your calendar
  • Use the app in English or German

Who it is for

Zettelman is useful for people who receive appointment slips from clinics, hospitals, practices, or offices and want a faster way to digitize them on iPhone without manually retyping every detail.
