What Zettel Scan does

  • Capture event info with your iPhone camera or from existing photos and screenshots
  • Extract date, time, location, title, and context automatically
  • Review and edit parsed details before saving
  • Create reminders and optionally add the event to your calendar
  • Use the app in English or German

High-intent use cases

  • Medical appointments and follow-up visits
  • School meetings and parent-teacher conferences
  • Embassy, visa, and government office appointments
  • Vehicle service and inspection bookings
  • Invitations, event flyers, and printed schedules

How it works

  1. Capture a photo or pick a screenshot that contains event details.
  2. Zettel Scan extracts the important fields automatically.
  3. Review, edit if needed, then save as reminder and optional calendar event.

FAQ

Does Zettel Scan only work for doctor slips?

No. It also works with email screenshots, invitations, letters, and other notes that include event details.

Can I edit extracted details before saving?

Yes. You can adjust parsed fields like date, time, location, and event title before you save.

Can it create both reminders and calendar events?

Yes. You can save a reminder and optionally add the same entry to your calendar.

Who it is for

Zettel Scan is for anyone who gets event information in daily life and wants to digitize it on iPhone quickly, accurately, and with much less typing.
